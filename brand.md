# TalentForge AI Brand Kit

## Brand Positioning
- **Name:** TalentForge AI
- **Tagline:** Portfolio Intelligence
- **Core Promise:** Turn GitHub proof-of-work into recruiter-ready career assets.

## Voice and Tone
- Confident, outcome-oriented, and evidence-driven.
- Clear and concise; avoid hype-only claims.
- Focus on recruiter value: impact, clarity, and readiness.

## Color Tokens
- **Primary Glow:** `--accent-glow`
- **Secondary Glow:** `--accent-glow-2`
- **Background:** `--background`
- **Foreground:** `--foreground`
- All values are managed in `src/styles.css`.

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter
- **Monospace/Technical Accent:** JetBrains Mono
- Heading hierarchy should emphasize proof, outcomes, and action.

## Identity Assets
- **Logo/Favicon:** `public/brandmark.svg`
- **Social Preview:** `public/og-talentforge.svg`
- **Manifest:** `public/site.webmanifest`

## UI Primitives
- `brand-wordmark`: headline/logo treatment
- `brand-kicker`: premium badge/chip style
- `brand-button`: primary CTA gradient + glow
- `hero-shell`: hero container gradient glass system

## Usage Rules
- Keep gradients subtle enough for readability.
- Ensure WCAG contrast for all major CTAs and text blocks.
- Preserve clear spacing around logo and primary CTA.
